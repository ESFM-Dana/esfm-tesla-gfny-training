Pre-Job Tailboard

  • Scope & area boundaries
  • PPE, chemicals, dilution, equipment
  • Barricade & floor protection plan
  • Traffic & timing windows
  • Waste handling rules
  • Success criteria & sign-off
  • Photos: before/after

PPE & Safety

  • Gloves (cut-resistant for glass/metal)
  • Eye protection; hearing as needed
  • Wet floor signs before water/chemicals
  • Respect barricades & exits
  • Follow SDS & dilution
  • Stop Work if unsafe/unclear

Core Steps (Examples)

  • Kaivac Deep Clean: Pre-spray → dwell → agitate → rinse/extract → polish & restock → QA
  • Top Scrub & Recoat: Dust mop → scrub → wet vac & rinse → thin coats (2–3) → QA
  • Spill Extraction: Set signs → extract outside-in → neutralize → accelerate dry → QA

Quality Gates

  • Check at 25% / 50% / 90%
  • Edges, corners, thresholds, finishes
  • Correct immediately; don’t defer

Completion

  • Clean/dry; uniform finish; no residue
  • Waste disposed correctly
  • Tools cleaned; chargers connected
  • Before/After photos
  • Lead/Manager sign-off
